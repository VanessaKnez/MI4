Why do we need your information?

  • First name and last name

    This will identify you to other users.


  • Email address

    With your email address you can login to our system as an unique user. This will identify as a user. We check in our system if this email address exists.


  • City and postal code

    The city where you live in will help other users to see if you are nearby or not. The city will be shown on a map.


  • Phone number

    Other users can contact you with your phone number. This information is not mandatory. You can choose if you fill this in or not. This will be shown on your ads if you do fill it in.


  • Password

    With your password we can identiy you as the real user if this matches with the right email address.
